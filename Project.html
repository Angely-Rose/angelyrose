Explorations


Outdoor Leadership

This is an exploration that enhances a lot of skills including wilderness survivals, medical and risk management, outdoor leadership, community building and more skills that goes on and on. What's special about this exploration is that it has the power to nurture the passion and strength of an individual to become an environmental stewards who is really connected with the nature.

Community Documentary

In each community, there are a lot of stories, of hopes, pains and the different perspectives of lives in different environmnents. In this project, I got to listen to all of the stories in four communities in Kampong Speu province. After gathering all of the stories, we started to document them in different forms, pictures, writings, videos and podcasts. When we brought these products and showed them to the community, the villagers were really empowered by how their voices can be portrayed by those documentaries.

Plastics (Machines)

Our generation is on the brink of a massive climate change that has never been seen in all human history before. Species are going extinct, why? A huge part of this polution is Plastics. To study more about plastics and sollutions we can implement to help it, this exploration has visited several organizations in Siem Reap that works to recycle plastics by using many new innovations that is new to our country. Their actions towards plastics were incredible and had greatly inspired me to pursue a greater future, too.

Community Projects


42k Project

After introduced to a Design Thinking Workshop, I was implementing my skills to help design a secured system to keep electrical devices for an education organizations. This project aim is to help support the rural education by securing the educational gadgets.

Cambodian Geography Book

To be able to spread sustainable impacts to the community, we need to come up with creative ways to share the impact. Therefore, I and other students initiated the Cambodian Geography Book that is currently on sales and contributed to government schools across the countries for students to read. We worked tirelessly for 2 years from interviewing people, to editing the content and editing those informations into an engaging book. I am proud of being a part of this project for it will inspire other students to see the beauty of their country.
