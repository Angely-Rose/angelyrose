I have always dream of changing my own country and making this world a better place.

My dream is to foster great Skills, Passions and Knowledge for young students in Cambodia. I believe they have great potential to change the world around them with their curiosity and skills. In a developing country, a great force we need is the human resources. Therefore, education for young people is the key to changing our country's future. Just like I was trusted in by Liger Leadership Academy to be a change agent, I want to continue this great spirits on to those capable childrens. I was just like them, until I found a place that my passions spark. In the future, I want those children to find their light, too. That is why my dream is to invest in those future leaders who needs help embracing their potential.

Entrepreneurship and Technology is the fields that has greatly inspire me and make me believe in a greater future. After joining competitions like Technovation Girls, First Lego League Robotics competitions and events like Barcamp, My dreams now lies with these fields even more.

I imagine, what if there is a place where all entrepreneurs come to work together, build innovative solutions and design creative products to help humanity develop in a generation that needs help the most?

What if you work in a place with ideas radiating around the room? With people, resources and companies who are there to help you? This lead me to dream of creating a working space, where innovation comes to work, ideas come together to create a bigger change in the world.
