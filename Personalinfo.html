Hi beautiful readers out there! Below are my personal information that can help you to know me better:

Birthday and Hometown

I was born on the 5th of October, 2005 (National Teachers Day) in Kratie Province. My hometown is right beside the Mekong River that flows through many countries in Southeast Asia.

Chhlong District, My Origin

My Hometown is a quiet and peaceful community. Most people there work as farmers because the delta land near the river is great for all kinds of agriculture. The common crops are smoking weeds, rice, corns and other crops. My hometown is a part of my identity, and I am proud to be born in such a peaceful community with such authentic lifestyles. The stories of Chhlong district will always inspire me and make me remember my origin.
